Works where you scroll

Instagram

Images, reels, and carousels at full resolution. Not the compressed preview you get from a screenshot.

Twitter / X

Photos at original upload quality. Profile pictures at full size instead of the tiny thumbnail.

Facebook

Images and videos from posts and pages. Grabs the highest quality version available on the CDN.

What you get

HD quality

Rewrites CDN URLs to fetch the highest available resolution, not the compressed preview.

Multi-image posts

Download every image from a carousel or gallery in one click. No swiping through one at a time.

Download history

Quick access to recent downloads from the popup. See what you grabbed and when.

Organized folders

Files saved to SocialSnag/instagram/, SocialSnag/twitter/, etc. Sorted automatically.

Platform toggles

Turn individual platforms on or off. Only activate what you use.

Zero tracking

No analytics, no telemetry, no server. Your browsing data stays on your device.

No tracking. No servers. No nonsense.

Everything stays on your device. There is no backend, no analytics, no data collection. Not even a server to phone home to. SocialSnag is just code running in your browser.

Developer mode

  1. Clone the repo: git clone https://github.com/jamditis/socialsnag.git
  2. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome
  3. Toggle on Developer mode in the top right
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the folder
  5. Right-click any image on a supported site
