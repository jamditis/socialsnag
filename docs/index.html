Works where you scroll

Instagram

Images, reels, and carousels at full resolution. Not the compressed preview you get from a screenshot.

Twitter / X

Photos at original upload quality. Profile pictures at full size instead of the tiny thumbnail.

Facebook

Images and videos from posts and pages. Grabs the highest quality version available on the CDN.

Bluesky

Images from posts at full resolution. Extracts the original upload from the AT Protocol CDN.

What you get

HD quality

Rewrites CDN URLs to fetch the highest available resolution, bypassing standard front-end media compression.

Multi-image posts

Download every image from a carousel or gallery in one click. No swiping through one at a time to grab everything.

Download history

Quick access to recent downloads directly from the extension popup.

Organized folders

Files are automatically sorted into SocialSnag/twitter/ and other dedicated folders.

Platform toggles

Turn individual platforms on or off from the options menu at any time.

Zero tracking

No analytics, no telemetry, no servers. Your browsing data stays on your device. All scraping and formatting runs locally inside your browser, meaning SocialSnag can't track what you download.

No tracking. No servers. No nonsense.

SocialSnag has no backend, no analytics, and no data collection servers. All media processing happens in your browser, and settings are stored using the browser's built-in extension storage. If you have browser sync enabled, your browser may sync those settings via its own cloud services — controlled by your browser account, not SocialSnag.
